A good undergraduate science reader should be clear, conceptually rich, reliable, and freely accessible in digital format. The following list brings together journals and magazines useful for students, teachers, and early researchers.

<div class="journal-grid">
  <article>
    <a href="https://www.scienceinschool.org/">Science in School</a>
    <p>General STEM articles, classroom resources, experiments, teaching ideas, and accessible explanations across school-to-undergraduate science.</p>
  </article>
  <article>
    <a href="https://kids.frontiersin.org/">Frontiers for Young Minds</a>
    <p>Accessible science articles written by researchers and reviewed for clarity by young readers.</p>
  </article>
  <article>
    <a href="https://www.quantamagazine.org/">Quanta Magazine</a>
    <p>Readable science articles on mathematics, physics, computer science, biology, complexity, and fundamental ideas.</p>
  </article>
  <article>
    <a href="https://www.ias.ac.in/Journals/Resonance_Journal_of_Science_Education/">Resonance</a>
    <p>Undergraduate-friendly science education journal across physics, chemistry, biology, mathematics, and engineering.</p>
  </article>
  <article>
    <a href="https://physics.aps.org/">APS Physics Magazine</a>
    <p>Modern physics explained through research highlights and accessible discussions of important results.</p>
  </article>
  <article>
    <a href="https://cerncourier.com/magazine/">CERN Courier</a>
    <p>Readable source for particle physics, accelerators, detectors, cosmology, and high-energy physics.</p>
  </article>
  <article>
    <a href="https://www.physedu.in/">Physics Education - IAPT India</a>
    <p>Physics education journal useful for conceptual explanations, laboratory experiments, and pedagogy.</p>
  </article>
  <article>
    <a href="https://plus.maths.org/content/">Plus Magazine</a>
    <p>Accessible explanations of mathematical ideas, mathematical physics, probability, patterns, and current research.</p>
  </article>
  <article>
    <a href="https://www.ams.org/publications/notices/notices">Notices of the AMS</a>
    <p>Advanced mathematical reading, essays, reviews, historical discussions, and professional commentary.</p>
  </article>
  <article>
    <a href="https://currentscience.info/">Current Science</a>
    <p>Research news, reviews, commentary, Indian science, and developments across many scientific disciplines.</p>
  </article>
  <article>
    <a href="https://science.nasa.gov/earth/earth-observatory/">NASA Earth Observatory</a>
    <p>Highly visual science reading based on satellite images, climate, weather, oceans, landforms, and environmental change.</p>
  </article>
  <article>
    <a href="https://www.nature.com/nature/articles">Nature</a>
    <p>Broad science journal useful for following research highlights, news, reviews, and major developments.</p>
  </article>
</div>

For regular undergraduate reading, a strong starting combination is **Resonance**, **Current Science**, **Science in School**, **APS Physics Magazine**, **CERN Courier**, **Plus Magazine**, and **Quanta Magazine**.
