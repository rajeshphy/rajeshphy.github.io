Q: The ground state energy of hydrogen is approximately
A: $-13.6\,\mathrm{eV}$
B: $+13.6\,\mathrm{eV}$
C: $-1.6\,\mathrm{eV}$
D: $0\,\mathrm{eV}$
ANSWER: A

Q: Electric dipole transitions require
A: $\Delta l=0$
B: $\Delta l=\pm1$
C: $\Delta m=\pm2$ only
D: $\Delta n=0$ only
ANSWER: B

Q: The total number of states in a shell with principal quantum number $n$, including spin, is
A: $n$
B: $n^2$
C: $2n^2$
D: $4n^2$
ANSWER: C
SOURCE: JEST 2022 style

Q: The Zeeman effect is splitting of spectral lines in
A: electric field
B: magnetic field
C: gravitational field
D: thermal field
ANSWER: B
SOURCE: GATE 2021 style

Q: The spin quantum number of an electron is
A: $0$
B: $1/2$
C: $1$
D: $3/2$
ANSWER: B

Q: For hydrogen, the wavelength of a Balmer line corresponds to transitions ending at
A: $n=1$
B: $n=2$
C: $n=3$
D: $n=\infty$

A: Lamb shift only
