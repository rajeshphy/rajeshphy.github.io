## Daily Theoretical Physics Paper Search

Use this focused prompt in Scholar Labs:

```text
What recent arXiv papers contain explicit analytic calculations in supersymmetric quantum mechanics, PT-symmetric or non-Hermitian quantum mechanics, Darboux transformations, shape-invariant potentials, similarity transformations, pseudo-Hermitian Hamiltonians, or exactly solvable Schrodinger systems?
```

Prefer papers containing explicit potentials, spectra, wave functions, factorization relations, intertwining operators, metric operators, algebraic constructions, or solvable examples.

Deprioritize experimental papers, numerical-only studies, machine-learning work, detector or instrumentation papers, observational studies, and broad phenomenology without reusable analytic calculations.

After selecting a paper, use this follow-up prompt:

```text
Explain why this paper is relevant to SUSY, PT-symmetric, non-Hermitian, or analytic quantum mechanics. Identify its main technique, one calculation worth reproducing, the required background vocabulary, and whether it is best suited to lecture notes, a postgraduate project, or research reading.
```
