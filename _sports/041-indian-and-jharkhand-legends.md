  <h3 class="investment-section-heading">Why This Chapter Matters</h3>
  <section class="sports-topic">
    <p>Indian and Jharkhand legends give you memory anchors. Use them to understand roles, eras, sporting identity, and public conversation.</p>
    <p>This chapter gives you the background that childhood sports followers usually collect slowly through repeated matches, arguments, highlights, and newspaper reports. Read it as a foundation, then attach the ideas to real sports news using the source links below.</p>
  </section>

  <h3 class="investment-section-heading">Core Material</h3>
  <section class="sports-topic">
    <p>MS Dhoni anchors cricket captaincy, finishing, wicket-keeping, and Ranchi identity. Deepika Kumari anchors Jharkhand archery and Olympic precision-sport pressure. Jaipal Singh Munda anchors hockey history and public leadership.</p>
    <p>Major Dhyan Chand anchors Indian hockey greatness. Sachin Tendulkar anchors batting legacy and cricket records. P. V. Sindhu anchors badminton, world championships, and Olympic medals. Mary Kom anchors boxing resilience. Neeraj Chopra anchors athletics and javelin vocabulary.</p>
    <p>Do not memorize every number first. Start with why each person matters, their sport, signature skill, and the words their career teaches.</p>
  </section>
