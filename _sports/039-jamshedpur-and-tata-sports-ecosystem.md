  <h3 class="investment-section-heading">Core Material</h3>
  <section class="sports-topic">
    <p>A sports ecosystem includes grounds, coaches, academies, scholarships, competitions, medical support, equipment, and jobs. Talent alone is not enough.</p>
    <p>Jamshedpur and Tata-linked sports structures are useful for understanding how industrial cities support football, athletics, archery, and other sports.</p>
