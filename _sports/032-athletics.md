  <h2 class="act-section-heading"><span>32</span>Athletics</h2>
  <p class="act-essence-box">Athletics is built on measurable performance: time, distance, height, qualification mark, personal best, season best, and final position.</p>

  <h3 class="investment-section-heading">Why This Chapter Matters</h3>
  <section class="sports-topic">
    <p>Athletics is built on measurable performance: time, distance, height, qualification mark, personal best, season best, and final position.</p>
    <p>This chapter gives you the background that childhood sports followers usually collect slowly through repeated matches, arguments, highlights, and newspaper reports. Read it as a foundation, then attach the ideas to real sports news using the source links below.</p>
  </section>

  <h3 class="investment-section-heading">Core Material</h3>
  <section class="sports-topic">
    <p>Track events are measured by time. Field events are measured by distance or height. Some events use heats, semifinals, and finals. Others use qualification rounds and finals.</p>
    <p>Personal best is an athlete&#x27;s best-ever mark. Season best is best in the current season. National record and world record compare wider history.</p>
    <p>For India, Neeraj Chopra made javelin vocabulary mainstream: run-up, release, legal throw, foul, qualification, final, and best attempt.</p>
  </section>

  <h3 class="investment-section-heading">Worked Example</h3>
  <section class="sports-topic">
    <p>Suppose a headline uses this topic but gives very little background. Do not begin by arguing. First identify the event and competition. Second identify the role of the main player, team, number, or condition. Third explain the consequence in one calm sentence. In this chapter, the first words to watch are heat, final, personal best.</p>
  </section>

  <h3 class="investment-section-heading">Common Mistakes</h3>
  <section class="investment-box investment-box-event">
    <ul>
      <li>Reading only the headline and missing the match context.</li>
      <li>Using a term in conversation before connecting it to a real score, table, player, or event.</li>
      <li>Treating one clip, one statistic, or one fan opinion as the full story.</li>
    </ul>
  </section>

  <h3 class="investment-section-heading">Vocabulary Bank</h3>
  <section class="investment-box investment-box-definition">
    <ul>
      <li><strong>heat</strong>: early race round</li>
      <li><strong>final</strong>: medal or title round</li>
      <li><strong>personal best</strong>: athlete&#x27;s best-ever performance</li>
      <li><strong>season best</strong>: best performance in current season</li>
      <li><strong>foul</strong>: invalid attempt</li>
      <li><strong>qualifying mark</strong>: standard needed to advance or enter</li>
    </ul>
  </section>
