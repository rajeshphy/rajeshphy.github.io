  <p class="act-essence-box">Field hockey is fast, structured, and important to Indian sports history. Learn quarters, circle entries, penalty corners, and pressing.</p>

  <h3 class="investment-section-heading">Why This Chapter Matters</h3>
  <section class="sports-topic">
    <p>Field hockey is fast, structured, and important to Indian sports history. Learn quarters, circle entries, penalty corners, and pressing.</p>
    <p>This chapter gives you the background that childhood sports followers usually collect slowly through repeated matches, arguments, highlights, and newspaper reports. Read it as a foundation, then attach the ideas to real sports news using the source links below.</p>
  </section>

  <h3 class="investment-section-heading">Core Material</h3>
  <section class="sports-topic">
    <p>Modern hockey is played in four quarters. Goals count only from inside the shooting circle. This makes circle entry and final touch crucial.</p>
    <p>Penalty corners are major scoring chances. A drag-flicker, stopper, injector, and defensive runners all have roles. Many reports mention penalty-corner conversion.</p>
    <p>India&#x27;s hockey history includes Olympic dominance and figures such as Jaipal Singh Munda and Dhyan Chand. Modern hockey adds fitness, rolling substitutions, pressing, and tactical structure.</p>
  </section>
