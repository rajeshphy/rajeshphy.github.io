  <h3 class="investment-section-heading">Core Material</h3>
  <section class="sports-topic">
    <p>Dhoni&#x27;s story helps connect Ranchi to international cricket. He is useful for learning how a player can be discussed as a batter, keeper, captain, finisher, and franchise leader.</p>
    <p>Study Dhoni through situations: chase pressure, final overs, field setting, wicket-keeping up to the stumps, DRS judgement, and leadership under noise.</p>
    <p>When Dhoni appears in news, the topic is often legacy, IPL role, captaincy memory, finishing, fitness, or transition from international career to franchise cricket.</p>
  </section>
