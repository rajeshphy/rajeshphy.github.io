 <p class="act-chapter-kicker">CHAPTER IV: AUTHORITIES OF THE UNIVERSITY</p>
 <h2 class="act-section-heading"><span>43</span>Academic Council</h2>
 <p class="act-essence-box">Brief: The Academic Council shall be the principal academic authority of the University, responsible for regulating and maintaining the standards of teaching, research and evaluation in the University. It shall also be responsible for laying down the academic policies for maintenance and improvement of standards of teaching, research, extension, collaborative programme in academic matters and evaluation of the workload of the, teachers, under policy guidance of Senate and Syndicate. The Academic Council shall...</p>
 <div class="act-section-body">
<p class="act-line act-line-main"><span class="act-marker">(1)</span><span class="act-line-text">The Academic Council shall be the principal academic authority of the University, responsible for regulating and maintaining the standards of teaching, research and evaluation in the University. It shall also be responsible for laying down the academic policies for maintenance and improvement of standards of teaching, research, extension, collaborative programme in academic matters and evaluation of the workload of the, teachers, under policy guidance of Senate and Syndicate.</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-main"><span class="act-marker">(2)</span><span class="act-line-text">The Academic Council shall meet as and when required, but not less than four times in a year.</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-main"><span class="act-marker">(3)</span><span class="act-line-text">The tenure for nominated members of Academic Council shall be three years.</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-main"><span class="act-marker">(4)</span><span class="act-line-text">The Academic Council shall consist of the following members, namely:Members</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(a)</span><span class="act-line-text">Vice Chancellor – Chairperson;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(b)</span><span class="act-line-text">Pro-Vice Chancellor;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(c)</span><span class="act-line-text">Financial Advisor;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(d)</span><span class="act-line-text">Dean (Academic Affairs);</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(e)</span><span class="act-line-text">Dean of each faculty;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(f)</span><span class="act-line-text">Controller of Examinations;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(g)</span><span class="act-line-text">Finance Officer;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(h)</span><span class="act-line-text">the Vice Chancellor shall nominate the following members, on seniority basis and also on the basis of additional criteria as may be prescribed by the Statutes:</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(i)</span><span class="act-line-text">three Principals of constituent colleges which are accredited by the National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC) or National Board of Accreditation (NBA), as the case may be, out of whom one shall be woman and one shall be a person belonging Scheduled Castes or Scheduled Tribes or PVTG:</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-proviso"><span class="act-marker">Provided that</span><span class="act-line-text">for the Universities as mentioned in Schedule-III of this Act, three Head of Departments shall be nominated on rotation basis, and</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(ii)</span><span class="act-line-text">two University professors, out of whom one shall be a person belonging Scheduled Castes or Scheduled Tribes or PVTG by rotation;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(i)</span><span class="act-line-text">two teachers, representing each faculty, with not less than fifteen years of teaching experience to be nominated by the Vice Chancellor, out of whom one shall be a person belonging Scheduled Castes or Scheduled Tribes or PVTG or Other Backward Class:</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-proviso"><span class="act-marker">Provided that,</span><span class="act-line-text">out of the teachers representing each faculty, under this clause, one shall be a woman;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(j)</span><span class="act-line-text">one nominated representative of the Syndicate;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(k)</span><span class="act-line-text">representative of Director, Higher Education not below the rank of Deputy Director;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(l)</span><span class="act-line-text">representative of Director, Technical Education not below the rank of Deputy Director;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(m)</span><span class="act-line-text">eight eminent experts from the institutes or organisations of national or state repute, such as Indian Institute of Technology, Indian Institute of Science Education and Research, Indian Institute of Management, Indian Space Research Organisation, Institute of Chartered Accountants of India, Institute of Cost Accountants of India, Institute of Company Secretaries of India, Indian Council for Social Research, Industrial Associations, Indian Olympic Association and allied fields and as much as possible representing all the faculties, nominated by Vice Chancellor;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(n)</span><span class="act-line-text">Director, Human Resource Management;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(o)</span><span class="act-line-text">Director, Student Affairs;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(p)</span><span class="act-line-text">Director, Inclusive, Education;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(q)</span><span class="act-line-text">an eminent person from Federation of Jharkhand Chamber of Commerce and Industries in the field of business and industry can be a member, as nominated Chancellor. by the Vice,</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(r)</span><span class="act-line-text">one member nominated by Jharkhand State Faculty Development Academy;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(s)</span><span class="act-line-text">one member nominated by Jharkhand State Higher Education Council;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(t)</span><span class="act-line-text">one member nominated by Jharkhand Council on Science, Technology and Innovation; and</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(u)</span><span class="act-line-text">Registrar – Member Secretary: At least one-third of the total members to be nominated by the Vice Chancellor shall be women, provided that, for Universities designated as Women Universities, three-fourth of the total members to be nominated by the Vice Chancellor shall be women.</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-main"><span class="act-marker">(5)</span><span class="act-line-text">The Academic Council shall have the following powers and duties, namely: —</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(a)</span><span class="act-line-text">to ensure that the University becomes a vibrant hub for teaching, learning, research and development, interactions and linkages with industries, cultivation of intellectual property rights and entrepreneurship and incubation of knowledge linked industries;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(b)</span><span class="act-line-text">to ensure in-demand market relevant courses are introduced in collaboration with industry having practical or hand-on training;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(c)</span><span class="act-line-text">to consider and approve with modifications, if any, the matters referred to it by the Board of Studies through the faculty;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(d)</span><span class="act-line-text">to frame curriculum as per latest norms of UGC;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(e)</span><span class="act-line-text">to seek opinion and recommendation from the JSFDA for regular updation of course structure, curriculum, pedagogy and delivery mechanism;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(f)</span><span class="act-line-text">to ensure that the spirit of research and entrepreneurship percolates to all colleges and recognized institutions of the University;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(g)</span><span class="act-line-text">to approve fees, other fees and charges as recommended by the fee fixation committee;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(h)</span><span class="act-line-text">to recommend to the institution of degrees, diplomas, certificates and other academic distinctions;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(i)</span><span class="act-line-text">to propose draft of University Ordinances relating to the academic matter to the Syndicate;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(j)</span><span class="act-line-text">to allocate Departments to the faculties;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(k)</span><span class="act-line-text">to prescribe qualifications and norms for appointment of paper-setters, examiners, moderators and others, concerned with the conduct of examinations and evaluation;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(l)</span><span class="act-line-text">to consider and make recommendations to the Syndicate for creation of posts of teaching and non-teaching staff, for self-financed courses only, as per the qualifications prescribed by UGC or concerned regulatory body, in concurrence with the Finance and Accounts Committee;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(m)</span><span class="act-line-text">to accord recognition to various certificate, diploma, advanced diploma and degrees programmes run by constituent or affiliated colleges, in consonance with the norms of the University Grants Commission and the State Government;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(n)</span><span class="act-line-text">to recommend to the Syndicate the comprehensive perspective plan of academics;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(o)</span><span class="act-line-text">to recommend to the conferment of autonomous status on affiliated or constituent colleges in accordance with the provisions of the Statutes;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(p)</span><span class="act-line-text">to approve new courses, inter-disciplinary courses and short-term training programmes referred to it by the concerned Board of Studies;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(q)</span><span class="act-line-text">to approve the, course syllabi16 paper-setters, examiners and moderators, and evaluation schemes of various courses recommended by the faculty concerned;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(r)</span><span class="act-line-text">to advise the Vice Chancellor, on all academic matters and submit financial feasibility report to the Syndicate on academic programmes;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(s)</span><span class="act-line-text">to work out the procedures, policies and practices to introduce more flexible approach to education and of ‘adaptive pace of learning’ with minimum and maximum duration for completion of a degree and other academic programmes;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(t)</span><span class="act-line-text">to ensure that the research projects are an integral part of curriculum for undergraduate and post-graduate programmes;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(u)</span><span class="act-line-text">to prepare academic calendar of the University for the subsequent academic year as per the guidelines of the University Grants Commission and the State Government, three months before the completion of the current academic year;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(v)</span><span class="act-line-text">to recommend to the Syndicate establishment of departments, colleges, centres, institutions of higher learning, research and specialized studies; and</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(w)</span><span class="act-line-text">to exercise such other powers and perform such other duties as may be conferred or imposed on it by or under this Act, the Statutes, University Ordinances and Rules.</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-main"><span class="act-marker">(6)</span><span class="act-line-text">The Academic Council shall refer all matters or decisions involving financial implications to the Syndicate for approval with due concurrence of Finance and Accounts Committee.</span></p>
  </div>
