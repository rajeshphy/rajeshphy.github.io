 <p class="act-chapter-kicker">CHAPTER XI: STATUTES, UNIVERSITY ORDINANCES AND UNIVERSITY RULES</p>
 <h2 class="act-section-heading"><span>121</span>Statutes</h2>
 <p class="act-essence-box">Brief: Subject to the provisions of this Act, the Statutes may provide for all or any of the following matters, namely; the institution of Fellowship, Scholarships, exhibitions, medals and prizes: the designations and powers of the officers of the University: the constitution, powers, functions and duties of the authorities of the University: the admission of educational institutions as colleges and the withdrawal of privileges from Colleges so admitted; the institution of Colleges and...</p>
 <div class="act-section-body">
<p class="act-line act-line-main"><span class="act-marker">(1)</span><span class="act-line-text">Subject to the provisions of this Act, the Statutes may provide for all or any of the following matters, namely;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(a)</span><span class="act-line-text">the institution of Fellowship, Scholarships, exhibitions, medals and prizes:</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(b)</span><span class="act-line-text">the designations and powers of the officers of the University:</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(c)</span><span class="act-line-text">the constitution, powers, functions and duties of the authorities of the University:</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(d)</span><span class="act-line-text">the admission of educational institutions as colleges and the withdrawal of privileges from Colleges so admitted;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(e)</span><span class="act-line-text">the institution of Colleges and hostels and their maintenance and management;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(f)</span><span class="act-line-text">the classification of teachers of the University, the manner of their appointment and their recognition;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(g)</span><span class="act-line-text">the constitution of pension, insurance or provident fund for benefit of the officers, teachers and other employees of the University, and Principal of Constituent College:</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(h)</span><span class="act-line-text">the maintenance of the register of registered graduates;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(i)</span><span class="act-line-text">the number, qualification, grade, pay, reservation of posts as per State Reservation Policy and conditions of service of officers and other employees of the University;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(j)</span><span class="act-line-text">the maintenance of accounts of the income and expenditure of the University including the income and expenditure of Colleges and the forms and registers in which such accounts shall be kept;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(k)</span><span class="act-line-text">the maintenance of Service Books of the employees of the University;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(l)</span><span class="act-line-text">the conferment of honorary degrees and distinctions; and</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(m)</span><span class="act-line-text">any matter which is to be prescribed by Statutes, or which is necessary to give effect to the provisions of this Act.</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-main"><span class="act-marker">(2)</span><span class="act-line-text">The State Government shall make Statutes to be followed by all Universities, unless otherwise prescribed.</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-main"><span class="act-marker">(3)</span><span class="act-line-text">In case of any need for Statutes or amendment thereunder, the University shall get the proposal of Statutes or amendment thereunder ratified by the Senate or Syndicate and send the same for approval of the State Government.</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-main"><span class="act-marker">(4)</span><span class="act-line-text">The State Government may approve or withhold approval thereto, of any proposed Statutes or amendment thereunder.</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-main"><span class="act-marker">(5)</span><span class="act-line-text">Notwithstanding anything contained in the previous sub-sections, the State Government shall have the power to prescribe Statutes or amendment thereunder on the subjects, as it may deem fit and such Statutes or amendment thereunder shall be binding upon the University.</span></p>
  </div>
