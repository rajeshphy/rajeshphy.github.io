 <p class="act-chapter-kicker">CHAPTER XIV: ESTABLISHMENT OF NEW UNIVERSITIES</p>
 <h2 class="act-section-heading"><span>137</span>Project Report</h2>
 <p class="act-essence-box">Brief: The Department of Higher and Technical Education shall prepare a detailed project report with the particulars, namely:- justification regarding the necessity of establishment of the proposed University; the name, location and headquarters of the proposed University; the objectives of the University; requirement of academic facilities including teaching and non-teaching staffs; availability of academic facilities including teaching and non-teaching staffs if any; the details of plans for campus development such as, construction of...</p>
 <div class="act-section-body">
<p class="act-line act-line-continuation">The Department of Higher and Technical Education shall prepare a detailed project report with the particulars, namely:-</p>
<p class="act-line act-line-main"><span class="act-marker">(1)</span><span class="act-line-text">justification regarding the necessity of establishment of the proposed University;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-main"><span class="act-marker">(2)</span><span class="act-line-text">the name, location and headquarters of the proposed University;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-main"><span class="act-marker">(3)</span><span class="act-line-text">the objectives of the University;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-main"><span class="act-marker">(4)</span><span class="act-line-text">requirement of academic facilities including teaching and non-teaching staffs;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-main"><span class="act-marker">(5)</span><span class="act-line-text">availability of academic facilities including teaching and non-teaching staffs if any;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-main"><span class="act-marker">(6)</span><span class="act-line-text">the details of plans for campus development such as, construction of buildings, development of structural amenities and infrastructure facilities and procurement of equipment for starting the University;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-main"><span class="act-marker">(7)</span><span class="act-line-text">the phased outlays of capital expenditure;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-main"><span class="act-marker">(8)</span><span class="act-line-text">the nature and type of programmes and courses of study, research and innovation proposed to be undertaken by the University;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-main"><span class="act-marker">(9)</span><span class="act-line-text">the nature of facilities proposed to be started;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-main"><span class="act-marker">(10)</span><span class="act-line-text">the nature of specialized teaching, training or research activities to be undertaken by the University including those related to the local needs, so as to fulfil its objects;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-main"><span class="act-marker">(11)</span><span class="act-line-text">the details of playgrounds and other facilities available or proposed to be created for games and sports and extracurricular activities like National Cadet Corps and National Service Scheme;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-main"><span class="act-marker">(12)</span><span class="act-line-text">proposed approach and initial plans for academic and research excellence, including accreditations to be sought and academic auditing; and</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-main"><span class="act-marker">(13)</span><span class="act-line-text">such other details as the Department of Higher and Technical Education may like to give.</span></p>
  </div>
