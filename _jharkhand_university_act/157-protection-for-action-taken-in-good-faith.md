 <p class="act-chapter-kicker">CHAPTER XV: JHARKHAND STATE UNIVERSITY SERVICE COMMISSION</p>
 <h2 class="act-section-heading"><span>157</span>Protection for Action Taken in Good Faith</h2>
 <p class="act-essence-box">Brief: No suit, prosecution or other legal proceeding shall lie against any member of the Commission for anything which is in good faith done or intended to be done in pursuance of the provisions of the Act.</p>
