 <p class="act-chapter-kicker">CHAPTER IV: AUTHORITIES OF THE UNIVERSITY</p>
 <h2 class="act-section-heading"><span>55</span>Board of Estate and Facility Management</h2>
 <p class="act-essence-box">Brief: This section establishes the structure and role of the Board of Estate and Facility Management. It identifies membership or composition where applicable, explains the body’s functions and decision-making responsibilities, and records procedural details such as meetings, quorum, nomination or reporting duties when the Act provides them.</p>


 <div class="act-section-body">
<p class="act-line act-line-main"><span class="act-marker">(1)</span><span class="act-line-text">The Board of Estate and Facility Management shall be the primary body responsible to ensure the formulation, adoption, implementation and monitoring of all initiatives or measures related to the campus infra development, safety and security of students, specifically, Women in the University Campus.</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-main"><span class="act-marker">(2)</span><span class="act-line-text">The Board of Estate and Facility Management shall consist of the following members,: -</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(a)</span><span class="act-line-text">Vice Chancellor - Chairperson;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(b)</span><span class="act-line-text">Pro-Vice Chancellor;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(c)</span><span class="act-line-text">Proctor;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(d)</span><span class="act-line-text">Registrar;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(e)</span><span class="act-line-text">Director, Inclusive Education;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(f)</span><span class="act-line-text">Director, Information Technology and Digital Learning;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(g)</span><span class="act-line-text">Director, Estate and Facility Management;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(h)</span><span class="act-line-text">Director, Student Affairs;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(i)</span><span class="act-line-text">Director, Constituent Colleges; and</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(j)</span><span class="act-line-text">Director, Estate and Facility Management - Member Secretary.</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-main"><span class="act-marker">(3)</span><span class="act-line-text">The Board of Estate and Facility Management shall have the following power and duties, namely:-</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(a)</span><span class="act-line-text">to ensure adherence with Rules and guidelines issued by University Grants Commission or any other statutory or regulatory body;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(b)</span><span class="act-line-text">to assess future space and facility needs for academic, administrative, and recreational purposes;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(c)</span><span class="act-line-text">to recommend strategies for sustainable campus development and environmental stewardship;,</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(d)</span><span class="act-line-text">to oversee the maintenance and operation of existing buildings, facilities, and infrastructure;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(e)</span><span class="act-line-text">to ensure compliance with, regulatory standards, codes, and University policies in construction and development projects;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(f)</span><span class="act-line-text">to monitor the allocation and expenditure of funds related to campus infrastructure and maintenance;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(g)</span><span class="act-line-text">to develop safety protocols including work place safety norms, fire safety and disaster management plans for campus facilities;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(h)</span><span class="act-line-text">to monitor and ensure that the healthcare facilities are properly maintained in the University and Constituent Colleges;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(i)</span><span class="act-line-text">to empanel the healthcare practitioners in the manner as prescribed by the Statutes;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(j)</span><span class="act-line-text">to carry out best practices study regarding campus security and safety of employees and students, inculcating the best practices or learnings from them;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(k)</span><span class="act-line-text">to release Annual reports of Universities including the data about the number of complaints of sexual harassment received, successfully disposed of and pending, without revealing the identities of complainants; and</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(l)</span><span class="act-line-text">to perform other such activities as prescribed by the Statutes.</span></p>
  </div>
