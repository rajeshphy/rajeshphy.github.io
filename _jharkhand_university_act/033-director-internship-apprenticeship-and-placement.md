 <p class="act-chapter-kicker">CHAPTER III: OFFICERS OF THE UNIVERSITY</p>
 <h2 class="act-section-heading"><span>33</span>Director, Internship, Apprenticeship and Placement</h2>
 <p class="act-essence-box">Brief: This section defines the office of the Director, Internship, Apprenticeship and Placement. It covers appointment or selection, term and reporting arrangements where stated, and sets out the powers, duties, vacancy arrangements, temporary-charge provisions or accountability requirements attached to the office under the Act.</p>


 <div class="act-section-body">
<p class="act-line act-line-main"><span class="act-marker">(1)</span><span class="act-line-text">The Director, Internship, Apprenticeship and Placement shall be an officer of the University responsible for managing the internship, apprenticeship and placement of the students of the University including their skill enhancement, capacity building and training.</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-main"><span class="act-marker">(2)</span><span class="act-line-text">The Director, Internship, Apprenticeship and Placement shall be appointed by the Vice Chancellor, from amongst the competent teachers having at least 12 years of experience.</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-main"><span class="act-marker">(3)</span><span class="act-line-text">The Director, Internship, Apprenticeship and Placement shall discharge his functions under the direction and guidance of the Board of Internship, Apprenticeship and Placement and shall be concerned with the implementation of the policies and directives given by the Board of Internship, Apprenticeship and Placement.</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-main"><span class="act-marker">(4)</span><span class="act-line-text">He shall be appointed for a term of three years or till the age of superannuation, whichever is earlier and may be reappointed for one more term of three years.</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-main"><span class="act-marker">(5)</span><span class="act-line-text">The Director, Internship, Apprenticeship and Placement shall report to the Vice Chancellor and shall exercise the authority commensurate with the following responsibilities: -</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(a)</span><span class="act-line-text">to develop and oversee internship, apprenticeship, and placement programs that align with academic curricula and industry needs;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(b)</span><span class="act-line-text">to collaborate with academic departments and integrate these programs into the curriculum effectively;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(c)</span><span class="act-line-text">to build and maintain, strong relationships with businesses, non-profits, government agencies, and other organizations to create and expand internship and placement opportunities;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(d)</span><span class="act-line-text">alumni, to engage with, faculty, Board of Apprenticeship Training (BOAT), Board of Apprenticeship Training (BOPT), and industry professionals and leverage new opportunities;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(e)</span><span class="act-line-text">to identify and engage potential employers for internship, apprenticeship and placement of students;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(f)</span><span class="act-line-text">to ensure that all internship, apprenticeship, and placement programs comply with University policies and industry regulations;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(g)</span><span class="act-line-text">to maintain accurate records of student placements, internships, and apprenticeships;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(h)</span><span class="act-line-text">to plan and execute career fairs, networking events, workshops and to connect students with potential employers for enhanced career readiness;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(i)</span><span class="act-line-text">to analyse program effectiveness and generate reports for University administration;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(j)</span><span class="act-line-text">to invite industry professionals to provide insights into career opportunities and industry trends;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(k)</span><span class="act-line-text">to gather feedback from students and employers to assess the effectiveness of programs and identify areas for improvement;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(l)</span><span class="act-line-text">to continuously evaluate and refine programs based on feedback, industry trends, and changing student needs;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(m)</span><span class="act-line-text">to monitor the establishment of placement cells in all constituent or affiliated colleges and the execution of their mandated activities; and</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(n)</span><span class="act-line-text">to provide information and other reports to the Board of Internship, Apprenticeship and Placement as required from time to time.</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-main"><span class="act-marker">(6)</span><span class="act-line-text">The Director, Internship, Apprenticeship and Placement shall exercise such other powers and perform such other duties, as may be prescribed by the Statutes.</span></p>
  </div>
