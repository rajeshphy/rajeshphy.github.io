 <p class="act-chapter-kicker">CHAPTER III: OFFICERS OF THE UNIVERSITY</p>
 <h2 class="act-section-heading"><span>37</span>Director, Constituent Colleges</h2>
 <p class="act-essence-box">Brief: This section defines the office of the Director, Constituent Colleges. It covers appointment or selection, term and reporting arrangements where stated, and sets out the powers, duties, vacancy arrangements, temporary-charge provisions or accountability requirements attached to the office under the Act.</p>


 <div class="act-section-body">
<p class="act-line act-line-main"><span class="act-marker">(1)</span><span class="act-line-text">The Director, Constituent Colleges shall be an officer of the University and shall be responsible for overseeing the administration and coordination of the University’s constituent colleges. He shall also ensure that Constituent Colleges are aligned with the University’s overall mission and strategic goals.</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-main"><span class="act-marker">(2)</span><span class="act-line-text">The Director, Constituent Colleges shall be appointed by the Vice Chancellor, from amongst teachers of the University not below the rank of Associate Professor.</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-main"><span class="act-marker">(3)</span><span class="act-line-text">The Director, Constituent Colleges shall discharge his functions under the superintendence, direction and guidance of the Board of Constituent Colleges and shall be concerned with the implementation of the policies and directives given by the Board of Constituent Colleges.</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-main"><span class="act-marker">(4)</span><span class="act-line-text">He shall be appointed for a term of three years or till the age of superannuation, whichever is earlier and may be reappointed for one more term of three years.</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-main"><span class="act-marker">(5)</span><span class="act-line-text">The Director, Constituent Colleges shall report to the Vice Chancellor and shall exercise the authority commensurate with the following responsibilities: -</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(a)</span><span class="act-line-text">to co-ordinate between the University administration and constituent colleges ensuring streamlined operations and consistent practices;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(b)</span><span class="act-line-text">to monitor and support the academic quality and performance of the constituent colleges, including program evaluations and accreditation processes;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(c)</span><span class="act-line-text">to implement and enforce University policies and procedures within the constituent colleges, including, those related to academic standards, student affairs, and faculty governance;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(d)</span><span class="act-line-text">and to establish monitor, performance metrics to assess the progress and impact of the Constituent Colleges initiatives;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(e)</span><span class="act-line-text">to oversee the deployment of teachers and non-teaching staff in Constituent Colleges in coordination with Director, Human Resource Management;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(f)</span><span class="act-line-text">to oversee the infrastructure requirement of Constituent Colleges and the implementation progress of various Schemes;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(g)</span><span class="act-line-text">to oversee the implementation of student centric services and support systems within the constituent colleges, including counselling, career services, and extracurricular activities;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(h)</span><span class="act-line-text">to facilitate partnerships and collaborations between constituent colleges and external organizations or institutions; and</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(i)</span><span class="act-line-text">to provide regular reports to the University administration on the performance, challenges, and achievements of the constituent colleges.</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-main"><span class="act-marker">(6)</span><span class="act-line-text">The Director, Constituent Colleges shall exercise such other powers and perform such other duties, as may be prescribed by the Statutes.</span></p>
  </div>
