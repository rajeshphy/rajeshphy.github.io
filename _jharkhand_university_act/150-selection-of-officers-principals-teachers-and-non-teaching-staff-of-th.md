 <p class="act-chapter-kicker">CHAPTER XV: JHARKHAND STATE UNIVERSITY SERVICE COMMISSION</p>
 <h2 class="act-section-heading"><span>150</span>Selection of Officers, Principals, Teachers and Non-Teaching Staff of the University</h2>
 <p class="act-essence-box">Brief: of Officers, Teachers Principals, and non-teaching Staff of the University The University shall send the requisition to the Commission for selection of Officers, Teachers and non-teaching staff and Principals of Constituent Colleges against the vacant post as per the roster approved by the State Government on or before 15th January of every year. The Commission shall accordingly advertise the vacancies received and carry out the selection process for the post of Officers...</p>
 <div class="act-section-body">
<p class="act-line act-line-continuation">of Officers, Teachers Principals, and non-teaching Staff of the University</p>
<p class="act-line act-line-main"><span class="act-marker">(1)</span><span class="act-line-text">The University shall send the requisition to the Commission for selection of Officers, Teachers and non-teaching staff and Principals of Constituent Colleges against the vacant post as per the roster approved by the State Government on or before 15th January of every year.</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-main"><span class="act-marker">(2)</span><span class="act-line-text">The Commission shall accordingly advertise the vacancies received and carry out the selection process for the post of Officers, Principals of Constituent Colleges, Teachers and non-teaching staff as per the eligibility criteria prescribed by the Statutes.</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-main"><span class="act-marker">(3)</span><span class="act-line-text">The Commission shall conduct, wherever necessary, examination and / or interviews and prepare a panel of suitable candidates for each advertised post by following the process as may be prescribed by the Statutes:</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-proviso"><span class="act-marker">Provided that,</span><span class="act-line-text">the selection process shall, wherever applicable, adhere, to the provision of the Jharkhand Competitive Examination (Measures for Control and Prevention of Unfair Means) Act23.</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-main"><span class="act-marker">(4)</span><span class="act-line-text">There shall be a Screening Committee for preliminary scrutiny of the applications. The composition of the Screening Committee shall be as follows: -</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(a)</span><span class="act-line-text">Secretary of the Commission – Chairperson;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(b)</span><span class="act-line-text">Deputy Secretary – Member; and</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(c)</span><span class="act-line-text">Controller of Examinations – Member.</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-main"><span class="act-marker">(5)</span><span class="act-line-text">The Screening Committee shall send the list of eligible candidates to the Selection Committee.</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-main"><span class="act-marker">(6)</span><span class="act-line-text">There shall be a Selection Committee for recommendation of candidates to the post of Officer, Teachers and non-teaching staff of the University and Principals of the Constituent College/s. The composition of the Selection Committee shall be as follows: -</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(a)</span><span class="act-line-text">Chairperson of the Commission – Chairperson;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(b)</span><span class="act-line-text">Member (Administration) – Member;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(c)</span><span class="act-line-text">All the three Members of the Commission – Member(s);</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(d)</span><span class="act-line-text">three experts in the subject concerned nominated by the Chairperson – Member(s);</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(e)</span><span class="act-line-text">an academician representing SC / ST/ OBC / Minority / Women / Differently abled categories to be nominated by the Chairperson – Member; and</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(f)</span><span class="act-line-text">Secretary of the Commission-Member Secretary.</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-main"><span class="act-marker">(7)</span><span class="act-line-text">The Selection Committee may constitute different panels, each headed by a Member of the Commission to conduct the interviews of the shortlisted candidates for each advertised post.</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-main"><span class="act-marker">(8)</span><span class="act-line-text">On completion of the selection process, the Commission shall send the post wise list of recommended candidates against the requisition provided by the University.</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-main"><span class="act-marker">(9)</span><span class="act-line-text">Every appointment of Officer, Teacher, or non-teaching staff and Principals of Constituent Colleges shall be made by the University only on the recommendation of the Commission, and any appointment made in contravention of such recommendation shall be null and void.</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-main"><span class="act-marker">(10)</span><span class="act-line-text">The decision of the Commission as to the eligibility or otherwise of a candidate for admission to any examination shall be final.</span></p>
  </div>
