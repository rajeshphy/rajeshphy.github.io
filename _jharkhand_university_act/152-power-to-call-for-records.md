 <p class="act-chapter-kicker">CHAPTER XV: JHARKHAND STATE UNIVERSITY SERVICE COMMISSION</p>
 <h2 class="act-section-heading"><span>152</span>Power to Call for Records</h2>
 <p class="act-essence-box">Brief: The Commission may call for any records, reports or other information from the University, if in its opinion, such records, reports or other information necessary for efficient discharge of its functions, and University shall furnish such records, reports or other information to the Commission.</p>
