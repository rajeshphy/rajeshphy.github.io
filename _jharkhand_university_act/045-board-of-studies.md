 <p class="act-chapter-kicker">CHAPTER IV: AUTHORITIES OF THE UNIVERSITY</p>
 <h2 class="act-section-heading"><span>45</span>Board of Studies</h2>
 <p class="act-essence-box">Brief: This section establishes the structure and role of the Board of Studies. It identifies membership or composition where applicable, explains the body’s functions and decision-making responsibilities, and records procedural details such as meetings, quorum, nomination or reporting duties when the Act provides them.</p>


 <div class="act-section-body">
<p class="act-line act-line-main"><span class="act-marker">(1)</span><span class="act-line-text">There shall be a Board of Studies for every subject or group of subjects prescribed by the Statutes. The Board of Studies shall be the primary academic body of the University.</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-main"><span class="act-marker">(2)</span><span class="act-line-text">The Board of Studies shall consist of the following members, namely: —</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(a)</span><span class="act-line-text">Dean of concerned Faculty - Chairperson;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(b)</span><span class="act-line-text">Head of the University department or institution in the relevant subject;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(c)</span><span class="act-line-text">six teachers having minimum ten years teaching experience, nominated by the Vice Chancellor in consultation with the Dean of the respective faculty, from the following categories, namely: —</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(i)</span><span class="act-line-text">one teacher from amongst the full-time teachers of the University departments in the relevant subject,</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(ii)</span><span class="act-line-text">two teachers from amongst the constituent colleges or institutions as applicable in the concerned subject, and</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(iii)</span><span class="act-line-text">three teachers from amongst affiliated colleges or institutions as applicable;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(d)</span><span class="act-line-text">one member nominated by Jharkhand State Faculty Development Academy;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(e)</span><span class="act-line-text">one member nominated by Jharkhand State Higher Education Council; and</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(f)</span><span class="act-line-text">one member nominated by Jharkhand Council on Science, Technology and Innovation. Senior most Head of the Department as mentioned in sub-section (2)(b), shall act as Member Secretary.,</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-main"><span class="act-marker">(3)</span><span class="act-line-text">The Board of Studies shall have the following powers and duties namely:-</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(a)</span><span class="act-line-text">to recommend through the faculty or faculties concerned and the Academic Council, the introduction of new certificate, diplomas and degrees as per industry requirements;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(b)</span><span class="act-line-text">to recommend to the through the faculty or faculties concerned and the Academic Council, the discontinuation of certificates, diplomas and degrees which have become irrelevant;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(c)</span><span class="act-line-text">to recommend to the faculty concerned, the course syllabi, course structures and evaluation schemes of various courses;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(d)</span><span class="act-line-text">to undertake skill gap analysis, market research, best practices study, etc. for introduction of in demand courses in line with the University vision to attain selfsustenance;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(e)</span><span class="act-line-text">to suggest extension programmes with respect to the courses introduced;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(f)</span><span class="act-line-text">to understand the requirements of industry or corporate or society at large and to incorporate them into the syllabi to make the teaching-learning process relevant to the needs of the time;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(g)</span><span class="act-line-text">to encourage learning by collaboration and participation by using information and communication technology tools;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(h)</span><span class="act-line-text">to design curricula, add vocational content to every discipline and to prescribe the minimum period to pursue skill development programme and the level of proficiency expected;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(i)</span><span class="act-line-text">to ratify curricula, all processes and practices developed by the autonomous colleges, University departments or institutions, autonomous recognized institutions, empowered autonomous colleges or cluster of institutions as applicable, and recommendations in respect of recognition of teachers or experts.</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(j)</span><span class="act-line-text">to seek recommendations or consultations from the JSFDA in the matters of course and curriculum design;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(k)</span><span class="act-line-text">to evaluate the feasibility of the self-financed course/s to be introduced by the concerned University Department or Constituent Colleges (as applicable);</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(l)</span><span class="act-line-text">coordinate with Academic Council and Finance and Accounts Committee for academic and financial concurrence before seeking approval of the syndicate for the concerned self-financed course; and</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(m)</span><span class="act-line-text">to monitor the operations of self-financed course/s and propose any modification, extension or closure of such course as required.</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-main"><span class="act-marker">(4)</span><span class="act-line-text">The Board of Studies shall constitute a Research and Recognition Committee for each University Department, -</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(a)</span><span class="act-line-text">The Research and Recognition Committee shall consist of the following members, namely:-</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(i)</span><span class="act-line-text">Head of the Concerned University Department- Chairperson,</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(ii)</span><span class="act-line-text">All teacher of the concerned University Department, and</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(iii)</span><span class="act-line-text">two experts in the subject, to be nominated by the Vice Chancellor, not below the rank of Professor, who have successfully guided at least three Doctorate of Philosophy (Ph.D.) students and have published research work in recognized or reputed national or international journals, anthologies, etc., one of whom shall be from outside the University;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(b)</span><span class="act-line-text">the Research and Recognition Committee shall have the following power and duties, namely: —</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(i)</span><span class="act-line-text">to approve the topic of thesis or dissertation in the subjectthe,</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(ii)</span><span class="act-line-text">to recommend a panel of referees for thesis or Vice Chancellor dissertations for awarding post-graduate, doctorate and higher degrees, based on the criteria as approved by the Academic Council,</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(iii)</span><span class="act-line-text">to recommend to the faculty, by following appropriate process, names of post-graduate teachers, research scientists in the recognized research and other institutions, active research and development experts having of not less than ten years’ experience in research and development laboratories or centres in variety of industries, for recognition as approved research guides, and</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(iv)</span><span class="act-line-text">to undertake any other task in academic and research matters, as may be prescribed by the Statutes or assigned to it by the Academic Council;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-main"><span class="act-marker">(5)</span><span class="act-line-text">The University shall follow the procedure for the award of PhD as prescribed by the Statutes.</span></p>
  </div>
