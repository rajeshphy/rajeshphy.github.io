 <p class="act-chapter-kicker">CHAPTER XV: JHARKHAND STATE UNIVERSITY SERVICE COMMISSION</p>
 <h2 class="act-section-heading"><span>148</span>Officers and Employees of the Commission</h2>
 <p class="act-essence-box">Brief: This section defines the office of the Officers and Employees of the Commission. It covers appointment or selection, term and reporting arrangements where stated, and sets out the powers, duties, vacancy arrangements, temporary-charge provisions or accountability requirements attached to the office under the Act.</p>


 <div class="act-section-body">
<p class="act-line act-line-main"><span class="act-marker">(1)</span><span class="act-line-text">Secretary, Deputy Secretary, Controller of Examinations, and Finance Officer shall be considered as Officers of the Commission.</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-main"><span class="act-marker">(2)</span><span class="act-line-text">All Officers of the Commission shall be appointed by the State Government with service conditions as may be prescribed by the Rules.</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-main"><span class="act-marker">(3)</span><span class="act-line-text">The Officers of the Commission shall be eligible for all such allowances as may be prescribed by the Rules.</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-main"><span class="act-marker">(4)</span><span class="act-line-text">Subject to such directions as may be issued by the State Government in this regard, the Commission may appoint such other employees as it may deem necessary for discharge of its duties under this Act and on such terms and conditions as may be prescribed by the Rules.</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-main"><span class="act-marker">(5)</span><span class="act-line-text">Officers and employees of the Commission shall be appointed and vest such powers and duties as may be prescribed by the Rules.</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-main"><span class="act-marker">(6)</span><span class="act-line-text">The State Government shall provide, Secretarial assistance to the Commission by way of providing the necessary Officers to assist the Commission in discharging its duties.</span></p>
  </div>
