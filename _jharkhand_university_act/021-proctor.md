 <p class="act-chapter-kicker">CHAPTER III: OFFICERS OF THE UNIVERSITY</p>
 <h2 class="act-section-heading"><span>21</span>Proctor</h2>
 <p class="act-essence-box">Brief: This section defines the office of the Proctor. It covers appointment or selection, term and reporting arrangements where stated, and sets out the powers, duties, vacancy arrangements, temporary-charge provisions or accountability requirements attached to the office under the Act.</p>


 <div class="act-section-body">
<p class="act-line act-line-main"><span class="act-marker">(1)</span><span class="act-line-text">The Proctor shall be the chief legal and compliance officer of the University, appointed by the Vice Chancellor, for, a period 03 years, from amongst such teachers of the University who are not below the rank of Associate Professor.</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-main"><span class="act-marker">(2)</span><span class="act-line-text">In case, and Professors, Associate Professors are less than six in number in the University concerned or if any of the Professor and Associate Professor is not willing to be appointed as Proctor, Assistant Professors having experience of more than fifteen years and having Ph.D. Degree may be considered for the appointment to the post of Proctor.</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-main"><span class="act-marker">(3)</span><span class="act-line-text">The Proctor shall be assisted by Deputy Proctor and Assistant Proctor in their mandated activities. The conditions of service of Deputy Proctor and Assistant Proctor shall be as prescribed by the Statutes.</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-main"><span class="act-marker">(4)</span><span class="act-line-text">The Proctor shall have the following roles and duties: -</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(a)</span><span class="act-line-text">responsible for maintaining discipline amongst students on campus, ensuring a conducive environment for learning and personal development;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(b)</span><span class="act-line-text">implement and enforce the University’s rules related to student conduct, ensuring consistent and fair application;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(c)</span><span class="act-line-text">investigate complaints and incidents of indiscipline, and recommend appropriate disciplinary actions in accordance with the University’s policies; and</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(d)</span><span class="act-line-text">exercise such other powers and perform such other duties as prescribed by the Statutes.</span></p>
  </div>
