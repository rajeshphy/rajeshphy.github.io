 <p class="act-chapter-kicker">CHAPTER II: STATE UNIVERSITIES</p>
 <h2 class="act-section-heading"><span>5</span>Powers and Duties of the University</h2>
 <p class="act-essence-box">Brief: The University shall have the following powers and duties, related to: — Teaching and Learning, namely as: - to impart instruction in such branches of learning as the University may think fit including professional studies and technology; to create and deliver well-structured, engaging, and up-to-date curricula; use diverse teaching methodologies to meet the needs of all learners; to incorporate the latest research findings into teaching practices; to equip students with critical thinking...</p>
 <div class="act-section-body">
<p class="act-line act-line-continuation">The University shall have the following powers and duties, related to: —</p>
<p class="act-line act-line-main"><span class="act-marker">(1)</span><span class="act-line-text">Teaching and Learning, namely as: -</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(a)</span><span class="act-line-text">to impart instruction in such branches of learning as the University may think fit including professional studies and technology;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(b)</span><span class="act-line-text">to create and deliver well-structured, engaging, and up-to-date curricula;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(c)</span><span class="act-line-text">use diverse teaching methodologies to meet the needs of all learners;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(d)</span><span class="act-line-text">to incorporate the latest research findings into teaching practices;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(e)</span><span class="act-line-text">to equip students with critical thinking, problem-solving, and professional skills;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(f)</span><span class="act-line-text">to ensure that teaching aligns with accreditation and institutional benchmarks;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(g)</span><span class="act-line-text">to collaborate across departments for Interdisciplinary Teaching;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(h)</span><span class="act-line-text">to carry out need assessment for establishment of Regional Center(s), Study Centre(s), and Community College(s)as, applicable within its jurisdiction;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(i)</span><span class="act-line-text">to promote an inclusive and equitable quality learning environment; and</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(j)</span><span class="act-line-text">to partner with external organizations to provide real-world learning opportunities. Collaboration, namely</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-main"><span class="act-marker">(2)</span><span class="act-line-text">Research and Collaboration, namely:-</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(a)</span><span class="act-line-text">to make provision for research and for the advancement and dissemination of knowledge, and generally to cultivate and promote the arts (including fine arts and performing arts), humanities, social sciences, accounts and commerce, pure and applied sciences, present and emerging technologies, managements, different forms of medicine, engineering, law, physical education and other branches of learning and culture and their multi-disciplinary and inter-disciplinary areas;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(b)</span><span class="act-line-text">to establish, maintain and manage departments, institutions of research, institutions of specialised studies;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(c)</span><span class="act-line-text">to provide for establishment of study centres, and also to provide for and maintain common resource centres in such Study Centres in the form of post-graduate departments, libraries, laboratories, computer centres and the like centres of learning and skills training, as per the guidelines laid down by the State Government or by the University Grants Commission (UGC);</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(d)</span><span class="act-line-text">to provide for the establishment of the University campus and / or study centres and / or Regional Centres depending on the administrative demand and requirement from students;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(e)</span><span class="act-line-text">to co-operate or collaborate with any other University, institution, authority or organisation for research and advisory services and for such purposes to enter into appropriate arrangement with other universities, institutions, authorities, or organisations to conduct certain courses as the situation may demand;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(f)</span><span class="act-line-text">to explore the possibilities of augmenting the resources of the University by exploring or innovating activities such as research and development, consultancy, training programme and providing services for different clients from industry, trade or any other non-government organisations;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(g)</span><span class="act-line-text">to undertake academic collaboration programmes, research and advisory services with Universities and institutions abroad, as per the regulations issued by UGC and related statutory bodies, from time to time with the vision of attaining self-sustenance;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(h)</span><span class="act-line-text">to undertake development programme in higher education, research, consultancybased projects and training programme for outside agencies, by charging fees, so as to generate internal funds and other resources;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(i)</span><span class="act-line-text">to create knowledge, disseminate it, and foster high quality research, which is contemporary, globally competitive and locally as well as regionally and nationally relevant;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(j)</span><span class="act-line-text">to strengthen education at under-graduate and post-graduate level, promote research culture in relevant degree programme and cultivate desire for entrepreneurship; and</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(k)</span><span class="act-line-text">to cultivate research parks, technology incubators and other engagement entities, to translate University research to commercial domain and coordinated projects involving multiple faculty groups from several disciplines that address some important issues before the State.</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-main"><span class="act-marker">(3)</span><span class="act-line-text">Appointment and Promotion of Employees, namely as: -</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(a)</span><span class="act-line-text">to make appointments to the posts of Officers, Directors, Principals, teachers, nonteaching staff and other posts of the University in the manner as prescribed by the Statutes;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(b)</span><span class="act-line-text">promotion of University employees as per procedure prescribed by the Statutes;arising</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(c)</span><span class="act-line-text">to analyze the vacancies, against the sanctioned posts for both teaching, nonteaching staffs and other posts of the University till 31st December of every year and preparation of thefor, roster timely appointment against such vacancies;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(d)</span><span class="act-line-text">to facilitate inter University transfer of employees as per the procedure prescribed by the Statutes;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(e)</span><span class="act-line-text">to establish Human Resource Development Centres (HRDCs) with the support of University Grants Commission (UGC) and other such arrangements for all-round training of its employees;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(f)</span><span class="act-line-text">to carry out induction training, career development program, skill enhancement program and workshops for the employees of the University in collaboration with Jharkhand State Faculty Development Academy;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(g)</span><span class="act-line-text">to periodically assess the performance of teachers in accordance with the norms prescribed by the University Grants Commission (UGC) and the Statutes;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(h)</span><span class="act-line-text">to periodically assess the performance of non-teaching employees in accordance with the norms prescribed by the Statutes;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(i)</span><span class="act-line-text">to collaborate with academic and research institutions for faculty exchange programs, joint collaborations, and research;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(j)</span><span class="act-line-text">to regulate and monitor the attendance of the employees on the premises of the University or College during teaching or working hours as prescribed; and</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(k)</span><span class="act-line-text">to enforce conduct and discipline rules for employees prescribed by the Statutes or Rules framed in this regard.</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-main"><span class="act-marker">(4)</span><span class="act-line-text">Internship, Apprenticeship and Placement of Students, namely as: -</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(a)</span><span class="act-line-text">to integrate internships as a mandatory component of certificate or diploma or degree or any other academic qualification;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(b)</span><span class="act-line-text">to provide sufficient internship and apprenticeship opportunities for all students through partnerships, agreements, memorandum of understanding with industry bodies;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(c)</span><span class="act-line-text">to establish dedicated board or office or cell focusing on internship, apprenticeship and placement of students;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(d)</span><span class="act-line-text">to identify, advertise and engage apprentices and advanced apprentices as per the requirement of the University through merit-based selection process as per the norms of Government of India or the State Government, as applicable;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(e)</span><span class="act-line-text">to introduce apprenticeship embedded degree programs with suitable industry partners as per the norms of University Grants Commission or any other regulating body as applicable;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(f)</span><span class="act-line-text">to introduce specialised courses for improving employability of students;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(g)</span><span class="act-line-text">to formulate policies for transparent merit-based placement of students;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(h)</span><span class="act-line-text">to identify skills to which students need to be exposed to, by taking into account the local needs, training facilities available, emerging needs and new employment opportunities; and</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(i)</span><span class="act-line-text">to regularly update the curriculum, ensuring employability of students.</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-main"><span class="act-marker">(5)</span><span class="act-line-text">Finances of the University, namely as: -</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(a)</span><span class="act-line-text">to borrow funds for the purposes of the University on the security of the property of the University, with the prior permission of the State Government;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(b)</span><span class="act-line-text">to receive funds for collaborative programme(s) from foreign agencies, subject to rules of the Central Government and the State Government in that behalf;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(c)</span><span class="act-line-text">to create development corpus out of surplus, that the University may generate through its teaching, learning, training, research, emerging technology, startup, innovations and development, consultancy and any other academic and support activities and to invest it in a professional manner, and use the interest generated through it for the growth and development of academic, research and development, academic and physical infrastructure development and any other infrastructure;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(d)</span><span class="act-line-text">to formulate strategic roadmap for generation of internal funds of the University by undertaking activities such as industry-academia collaboration, research and consultancy projects, alumni contribution and endowments, internationalization of education, intellectual property and technology transfer, start-up promotion and incubation, etc.;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(e)</span><span class="act-line-text">to introduce or operate or wind-off self-financed courses out of the internal funds of the University and carry out recruitments and appointments of teaching and nonteaching employees, if any for such courses in the manner as prescribed;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(f)</span><span class="act-line-text">to incur expenditure from various funding agencies within the Country without any share or contribution from the State Government, and contributions received from individuals, industries, institutions, organizations or any person whosoever, to further the objectives of the University, and contributions or fees for academic or other services offered by the University for aided and self-supporting academic programmes; and</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(g)</span><span class="act-line-text">to incur expenditure from the development fund, or any other fund established by the University for the purposes of creation of posts in various cadres, granting pay and allowances and other benefits to the posts created through its own funds provided those posts are not held by such persons, who are holding the posts for which government contribution is received, starting any academic programmes on self-financing basis, granting remunerations or incentives to its employees for performing any task assigned to them other than their regular duties and responsibilities and incurring expenditure on any development work and on welfare activities of its students and employees.</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-main"><span class="act-marker">(6)</span><span class="act-line-text">Promotion of Inclusive Education, namely as: -</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(a)</span><span class="act-line-text">to make special provisions for the benefit of University education to be made available to classes and communities which are socially and educationally backward or women students or divyangjan students or students from rural and tribal areas;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(b)</span><span class="act-line-text">to introduce bridge courses for students belonging to underprivileged sections of the society;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(c)</span><span class="act-line-text">to promote remedial courses for the academically weaker students; and</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(d)</span><span class="act-line-text">to take appropriate infrastructural or technological or social measures for complete safety of female teachers, non-teaching staff and students.</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-main"><span class="act-marker">(7)</span><span class="act-line-text">Operations of a University, namely as: -</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(a)</span><span class="act-line-text">to organize, maintain and manage University departments, institutions, laboratories, learning resource centres, libraries, museums and equipment for teaching, learning, training, research and development or extension;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(b)</span><span class="act-line-text">to establish, maintain and manage hostels, health centres, auditoria, student resource centres gymnasiums and other student related activities;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(c)</span><span class="act-line-text">to establish requisite infrastructure and engage personnel for campus security and safety of students and employees;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(d)</span><span class="act-line-text">to upgrade selected Constituent Colleges into Regional Centres of the University for decentralization of administrative and extension activities in the jurisdiction determined by the University;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(e)</span><span class="act-line-text">to prescribe the courses, of instruction and studies for the various examinations leading to specific degrees and diplomas or certificates;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(f)</span><span class="act-line-text">to make provision, wherever feasible, in the University departments, colleges and institutions, for survey and collection of statistics, data and other particulars relevant to various developmental activities including State and National plans, evaluation of the developmental schemes with the participation of the students as a part of their curricular activities;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(g)</span><span class="act-line-text">to supervise, control and regulate admission of students for various courses of study in University departments, Constituent and affiliated Colleges;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(h)</span><span class="act-line-text">to prepare the academic calendar and ensure its strict adherence;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(i)</span><span class="act-line-text">to confer honorary degrees or other academic distinctions as prescribed by the Statutes;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(j)</span><span class="act-line-text">to maintain discipline and cordial environment within the University;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(k)</span><span class="act-line-text">to make provision for participation of students in any programme, services or activities directed towards cultural, economic and social betterment as may be necessary and possible, to fulfill the objects of the University by the State Government or / and the Government of India;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(l)</span><span class="act-line-text">to promote by itself, or in co-operation with other universities or organisations, the study of regional, national and foreign languages;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(m)</span><span class="act-line-text">to evolve an operational scheme for ensuring accountability of its employees;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(n)</span><span class="act-line-text">to have a learner-centric approach and perform the role of being a knowledge creator;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(o)</span><span class="act-line-text">to create a comprehensive digital University framework for both, e- learning and eadministrative services;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(p)</span><span class="act-line-text">to exploit the power of ‘learning by collaboration’ and ‘participation’ with use of information and communication technology;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(q)</span><span class="act-line-text">to provide an environment for the all-round development of youth by exposing them to the rich cultural heritage of the country and creating opportunities for development of skills in sports;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(r)</span><span class="act-line-text">to comply with and carry out any directives issued by the State Government, from time to time, with reference to above powers, duties and responsibilities of the University;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(s)</span><span class="act-line-text">to conduct academic audit of University departments and related units at regular intervals;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(t)</span><span class="act-line-text">to confer and award such degrees, diplomas and certificates, and provide for such lectures, instruction and training for external students, and the students under correspondence and distance education, online and continuing education courses;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(u)</span><span class="act-line-text">to ensure that Green Audits of the University and its Constituent Units are conducted annually to achieve highest level of energy efficiency;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(v)</span><span class="act-line-text">to do all such other acts and things as may be necessary for, or incidental or conducive to, the attainment of all or any of its objects; and</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(w)</span><span class="act-line-text">to carry out the duties as mentioned under sub-section(1) to sub-section(7) of this section, the University may consult with JSFDA/ JSHEC/ JCSTI as required.</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-main"><span class="act-marker">(8)</span><span class="act-line-text">The Universities enlisted in Schedule II of this Act The Universities enlisted in Schedule II of this Act have been entrusted with additional responsibilities and powers, namely as: -</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(a)</span><span class="act-line-text">to make provisions to enable establishment of constituent and affiliated colleges to undertake specialised studies;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(b)</span><span class="act-line-text">to make provisions for creation of autonomous, empowered autonomous and empowered autonomous, cluster of institutions;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(c)</span><span class="act-line-text">to establish Study Centres or Regional Centres of the University as per its requirements and with clear geographical jurisdiction and assigned responsibilities;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(d)</span><span class="act-line-text">to hold or examinations, evaluations and confer certificates, diploma, degrees and postgraduate diplomas other academic distinctions on persons who, have pursued approved courses of study in the University or in a college and have passed the examinations and earned the required credits or marks or grades prescribed by the University;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(e)</span><span class="act-line-text">to lay down the conditions of affiliation of colleges and recognition of institutions taking into account the credibility of the management and the norms of academic performance of colleges, faculties and subjects, as may be laid down, from time to time, by the Statutes or Rules;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(f)</span><span class="act-line-text">to admit to the privileges of the University, affiliated or constituent colleges and institutions not maintained by the University and withdraw all or any of those privileges, temporarily or permanently;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(g)</span><span class="act-line-text">to designate constituent college, an affiliated college, institution as an autonomous constituent college, affiliated college or institution, in a stand-alone or cluster form as the case may be, in accordance with the guidelines, as prescribed by the Statutes;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(h)</span><span class="act-line-text">to supervise, control and regulate admission of students for various courses of study in constituent and affiliated colleges;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(i)</span><span class="act-line-text">to monitor and evaluate the academic performance of University departments, University institutions, constituent colleges and affiliated colleges, autonomous or empowered colleges in a stand-alone or cluster form for affiliation or recognition, as the case may be, for periodical State, National and World accreditations/ rankings;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(j)</span><span class="act-line-text">to hold and to manage trusts and endowments and to institute and award fellowship, travelling fellowship, scholarship, studentship, medals and prizes for teachers and students of the University and colleges;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(k)</span><span class="act-line-text">to inspect, where necessary, all types of colleges or institutions through suitable machinery established for the purpose, and take measures to ensure that proper standards of instruction, teaching, learning, training and research, and extension are maintained by them and adequate library, classrooms, laboratory, hostel, workshop and other academic facilities are provided for;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(l)</span><span class="act-line-text">to rescind or suspend affiliation or recognition or empowered status granted to colleges or institutions or cluster of institutions;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(m)</span><span class="act-line-text">to transfer the management of an affiliated college, institution or autonomous college or empowered autonomous college or cluster of institutions in case where irregularities or commissions or omissions of criminal nature by the management of such college or institution or mismanagement of such college or institution are prima- facie evident, to any other management, following proper legal procedure and with prior approval of the State Government; and</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(n)</span><span class="act-line-text">to evolve an operational scheme for ensuring accountability of teachers, nonvacational academic and non-teaching staff of the University, institutions and colleges.</span></p>
