 <p class="act-chapter-kicker">CHAPTER III: OFFICERS OF THE UNIVERSITY</p>
 <h2 class="act-section-heading"><span>34</span>Director, Estate and Facility Management</h2>
 <p class="act-essence-box">Brief: This section defines the office of the Director, Estate and Facility Management. It covers appointment or selection, term and reporting arrangements where stated, and sets out the powers, duties, vacancy arrangements, temporary-charge provisions or accountability requirements attached to the office under the Act.</p>


 <div class="act-section-body">
<p class="act-line act-line-main"><span class="act-marker">(1)</span><span class="act-line-text">The Director, Estate and Facility Management shall be an officer of the University responsible for handling all the activities related to infrastructure development, maintenance and up keeping of the University and its utilities.</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-main"><span class="act-marker">(2)</span><span class="act-line-text">The Director, Estate and Facility Management shall be appointed by the Vice Chancellor, from amongst teachers at the University, not be below the rank of Associate Professor or having teaching experience of at least 12 years.</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-main"><span class="act-marker">(3)</span><span class="act-line-text">The Director, Estate and Facility Management shall discharge his functions under the direction and guidance of the Vice Chancellor and implement the policies and directives given by the Building and Works Committee.</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-main"><span class="act-marker">(4)</span><span class="act-line-text">He shall be appointed for a term of three years or till the age of superannuation, whichever is earlier and may be reappointed for one more term of three years.</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-main"><span class="act-marker">(5)</span><span class="act-line-text">The Director, Estate and Facility Management shall report to the Vice Chancellor and shall exercise the authority commensurate with the following responsibilities: -</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(a)</span><span class="act-line-text">to ensure maintenance of the infrastructure facilities of the University and its units such as classroom, laboratories, hostels, student resource centres, health centres for employees and students, multipurpose hall, playground, libraries, etc.;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(b)</span><span class="act-line-text">to ensure that all infrastructure facilities of the University and its units adhere to the Building Code mandated by the Central Public Works Department or Department of Building Construction, Government of Jharkhand, as applicable;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(c)</span><span class="act-line-text">to ensure timely and regular safety audits of the Buildings in the University and its units by the concerned authorities;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(d)</span><span class="act-line-text">to oversee security systems and protocols, ensuring the safety and security of campus buildings and occupants;</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(e)</span><span class="act-line-text">to manage and upkeep all utilities being used by the University and ensuring its timely payment; and</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-sub"><span class="act-marker">(f)</span><span class="act-line-text">to ensure availability of doctors and medical personnel as required, and first-aid are placed at prominent places of the University, University Departments, Constituent Colleges, Hostels, Student Resource Centre, etc.</span></p>
<p class="act-line act-line-main"><span class="act-marker">(6)</span><span class="act-line-text">The Director, Estate and Facility Management shall exercise such other powers and perform such other duties, as may be prescribed by the Statutes.</span></p>
  </div>
